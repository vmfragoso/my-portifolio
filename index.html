Behind the scenes — how it was built
The Challenge

"Papelaria Amores" is a creative stationery brand that relies heavily on Instagram to showcase its products and connect with its audience. The company needed clear visibility into their content performance to drive strategic marketing decisions. The client, a non-technical business partner, required an automated way to track deep engagement metrics and receive actionable insights without the friction of manual data extraction or navigating complex software.

The Architecture & Stack

I designed an end-to-end data pipeline focused on automated ingestion, custom transformation, and AI-driven intelligence. The architecture ensures zero maintenance and a seamless handoff:

  • n8n: Orchestration and ingestion.
  • JavaScript: Custom data transformation and metric calculation logic embedded directly within the pipeline.
  • Instagram Graph API: Data source (Instagram Business Login auth, no Facebook Page required).
  • Google Sheets: Used for technical scope demonstration and metric collection for future reference, rather than serving as the primary data storage layer.
  • Google Looker Studio: Decision-making dashboard with real-time updates, interactive filters, and shareable delivery for non-technical stakeholders.
  • Claude: Architectural decisions, debugging, and mockup generation for stakeholder validation.
  • Claude Opus 4.7 AI Agent: Automated insight generation and content recommendations derived from the collected metrics.
How It Works
  • Data Ingestion & Transformation: The n8n workflow pulls raw data directly from the Graph API. Embedded JavaScript nodes immediately process this data, calculating crucial decision-making metrics such as Average Engagement Rate, retention time for Reels, and save-to-view ratios.
  • AI-Powered Analysis: Once the metrics are treated and calculated, the results are verified by an integrated AI agent powered by Claude Opus 4.7. This agent periodically reviews the data and generates automatic recommendations—completely replacing the manual analysis previously required to produce business insights.
  • Visualization: The processed data flows into a Looker Studio dashboard, making the metrics instantly accessible and visual.
Business Impact

By shifting the focus to concrete engagement metrics—like tracking saves to measure intent-to-purchase, or shares to gauge viral reach—the stakeholder can instantly identify what content formats actually work. Coupled with the Claude Opus 4.7 agent providing automated strategic recommendations, the client can make fast, data-driven decisions on their content strategy with zero technical friction.
